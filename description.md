# Saalt Group of Hotels - Website Documentation

## Overview

Saalt Group of Hotels is a comprehensive hotel booking and hospitality website featuring 4 premium properties across Bihar and Jharkhand, India. The website offers a complete digital presence with 20+ pages covering accommodations, events, corporate services, pilgrimage tourism, and more.

**Established:** 2019
**Properties:** 4 hotels/resorts
**Locations:** Patna, Bodh Gaya, and Jasidih (Deoghar)
**Contact:** +91 9709633313 | info@saalthotels.com
